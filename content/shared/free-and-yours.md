# About the cost

Both [[w:GitHub|GitHub]] and [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com) have generous free tiers that comfortably cover an ordinary wiki — you won't be asked for a card to get started. A very large or very busy wiki may eventually want a paid plan, but most never need one. And because your pages are plain files in your own GitHub account, your content is always yours to export or move.
