# About the cost

Running an ordinary wiki costs nothing. A wiki hosted on wikigit.org runs on its free hosting, and if you keep your pages in your own [[w:GitHub|GitHub]] account, that's free too. A very large or very busy wiki may eventually want a paid arrangement, but most never need one. And because your pages are plain files you own, your content is always yours to export or move.
